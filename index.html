FAO: 6th Form Leavers. – Please READ…

Please upload anything from your Computer User Area (N Drive, MyWork) you wish to keep to your school google account… this might be coursework, CV’s, Personal Statements etc as you won’t be able to access them when the exams finish and no longer attending the school.

Your School google account will be active until the October25 Half term, this gives plenty of time to download anything you wish to keep, after this date your school google account will deactivated.

Good luck with the external exams.

USB Storage Devices

Memory sticks are blocked, you are to use your own School Google Drive storage for bringing in and taking work home with you.

They are blocked to try and prevent work from being lost due to a failed or lost memory stick.

 Free copies of Microsoft Office for every student!

Would you like a free Office 365 account? Normally it’s £7.99 a month but it’s free to all students of Cheam High School!

You can install Office on up to 5 devices in total. They can be running Windows, OS X, iOS or Android. So, for example, you could install it on a couple of laptops at home, a desktop, an iPad and your phone. All for free!

Visit ICT Support before school, at break, lunchtime or after school to be given your account details.
